# /polis

Share the community's Polis survey for gathering sentiment.

## What to do

When the user runs `/polis`:

1. Read the Polis URL from the CLAUDE.md governance section
2. Share the link
3. Briefly explain what Polis is: "Polis helps the community find areas of agreement and disagreement. Your responses are anonymous and help shape governance decisions."
4. If results are available, share a brief summary

## Example output

```
Polis survey: [url]

Polis helps find where the community agrees and disagrees.
Your responses are anonymous. Results inform governance decisions.
```
