# Food & Dining

- Where: Main dining area, Building A
- Notes: Self-serve. Coffee available from 6:30am.

### Lunch
- When: 12:30pm - 2:00pm
- Where: Main dining area
- Notes: Varies daily. Check the board in the kitchen.

### Dinner
- When: 7:00pm - 9:00pm
- Where: Main dining area
- Notes: Communal dinner. Everyone welcome. Vegetarian option always available.

## Kitchen Access
Open to all residents 6am-11pm. Clean up after yourself. Shared supplies in the pantry (oil, salt, spices, rice, pasta). Label personal food in the fridge.

## Dietary Notes
Please let the kitchen team know about allergies or dietary restrictions. We always have vegetarian and vegan options at communal dinners.
