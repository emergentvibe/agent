# Community Norms

## Quiet Hours
10pm - 8am. No amplified sound. Keep voices down in shared spaces.

## Shared Spaces
Clean up after yourself. Don't leave personal items in common areas overnight.

## Communication
Default channel is the Telegram group. For urgent issues, contact an organizer directly.

## Guests
Residents can bring guests to common areas during the day. Overnight guests need to be registered with the operations team.

## Conflict
Talk to the person first. If that doesn't work, ask someone you both trust to help. The community bot can connect you with someone who's dealt with similar situations.
