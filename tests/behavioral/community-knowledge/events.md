# Events

## Recurring Events

### Morning Yoga
- When: Daily, 7:30am
- Where: Garden terrace
- Who: Open to all
- Notes: Bring your own mat. Maria usually leads.

### Weekly Community Dinner
- When: Every Friday, 7pm
- Where: Main dining area
- Who: Everyone
- Notes: Rotating cooks. Sign up on the kitchen board.

### Open Mic Night
- When: Every other Saturday, 8pm
- Where: Garden terrace
- Notes: Bring instruments, poetry, whatever. No sign-up needed.

## How to Propose an Event
Just ask in the group chat. If people are interested, it happens. No formal process needed.
